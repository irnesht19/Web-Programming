Vision

In 2022, becoming a leading center of education and advancement for science and information technology in Indonesia, which focuses on mobile and IoT application development, and becoming globally competitive, as well as be active in supporting industry and entrepreneurship.

Mission

  1. To perform high-quality education and teaching in science and information technology that focuses on mobile and IoT application development.
  2. To carry out research and improvement in science and Information Technology that focuses on mobile and IoT application development.
  3. To carry out social service and empowerment in the field of Information Technology.
  4. To cooperate with industries, companies, government, and other institutions in the field of Information Technology.
  5. To promote the spirit of entrepreneurship for students focusing on technopreneurship.
